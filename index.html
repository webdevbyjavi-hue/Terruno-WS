Who We Are

A Name Rooted
in the Land

Terruno was born from a simple truth: Mexico's vineyards and distilleries produce extraordinary things the world has yet to discover. Founded in 2023 by Daniel Miranda, we exist to change that — one carefully chosen bottle at a time.

The name speaks our purpose. Terruno derives from terra — land, soil, the living ground beneath every vine. It is the essence of terroir: the climate, the altitude, the centuries of tradition that make each provenance irreplaceable and every story worth sharing.
